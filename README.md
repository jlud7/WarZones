# War Zones - Advanced Naval Combat

A multi-layer strategic combat game inspired by Battleship, featuring four distinct battle zones: Space, Sky, Sea, and Underwater.

## Features

- **4 Battle Layers**: Space, Sky, Sea, and Underwater zones with unique visual themes
- **AI Opponent**: Intelligent AI with strategic decision-making
- **Power-ups System**: Discover treasures in underwater zones to unlock special abilities:
  - **Black Box**: Place an additional jet in your sky layer
  - **Krypton Laser**: Attack the same cell position across all four layers
  - **Cannon Ball**: Attack a 2Ã—2 area on the sea layer
- **Two Player Mode**: Play locally against a friend
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Score Tracking**: Track wins, hits, and accuracy across games

## How to Play

1. Choose "Play vs AI" or "Play vs Human" from the main menu
2. Place your ships on each layer (follow the on-screen instructions)
3. Take turns attacking opponent positions
4. Find treasures in the submarine layer to unlock powerful abilities
5. Sink all enemy ships to win!
